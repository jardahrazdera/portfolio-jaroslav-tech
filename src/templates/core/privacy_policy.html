Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last updated: September 2024

Information We Collect

Newsletter Subscription

When you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect:

  • Email address: Required to send you newsletter content
  • First name: Optional, used for personalization
  • Subscription date: For record keeping and analytics
  • IP address: For security and spam prevention

Website Analytics

We may collect basic website usage information including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Browser type and device information
  • Referring websites
  • General geographic location (country/city level)

How We Use Your Information

Newsletter

  • Send you blog post updates and newsletter content
  • Provide subscriber-only content and resources
  • Improve our content based on subscriber interests

Website Improvement

  • Analyze website usage to improve user experience
  • Monitor website performance and security
  • Understand which content is most valuable to visitors

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Service providers: Trusted services that help us operate the website (e.g., email delivery, hosting)
  • Your consent: When you explicitly agree to share information

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Secure HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure database storage with regular backups

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Unsubscribe: Opt out of newsletter communications at any time
  • Data portability: Request your data in a portable format

Newsletter Specific Information

Double Opt-In Process

We use a double opt-in process for newsletter subscriptions. This means:

  1. You enter your email address and consent to receive emails
  2. We send you a confirmation email
  3. You must click the confirmation link to activate your subscription
  4. Only then will you receive newsletter content

Unsubscribing

You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time by:

  • Clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any newsletter email
  • Contacting us directly at the email address below

Upon unsubscribing, we will stop sending you newsletters but may retain your email address to prevent accidental re-subscription.

Cookies and Tracking

This website uses minimal cookies and tracking:

  • Essential cookies: Required for website functionality (e.g., language preferences)
  • Analytics: Basic usage statistics to improve the website
  • No advertising cookies: We do not use advertising or social media tracking

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • For significant changes, we will notify newsletter subscribers via email
  • Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy

Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to exercise your rights, contact us:

Email: privacy@jaroslav.tech
